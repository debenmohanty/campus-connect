Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and allow us to improve our services.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Authentication Cookies: To keep you logged in and secure
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how users interact with our platform
  • Marketing Cookies: To show you relevant content and advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our platform:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period of time
  • First-party Cookies: Set by our website
  • Third-party Cookies: Set by our service providers

4. Cookie Management

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View the cookies stored on your device
  • Delete specific cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from being set
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies

5. Specific Cookies We Use

Here are some specific cookies we use on Campus Connect:

  • auth_token: Keeps you logged in to your account
  • user_preferences: Stores your language and display preferences
  • session_id: Maintains your session while using the platform
  • analytics_id: Helps us understand platform usage patterns

6. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may set cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media platforms for sharing features
  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Advertising networks for relevant content

7. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we will ask for your consent to use cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Change your preferences at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
